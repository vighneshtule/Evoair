MACHINE FEATURES

  • LIVE DATA SYSTEM

    • Real-Time Monitoring – Track sales and machine status instantly.
    • Advanced Analytics – Driven insights optimize sales and inventory.
    • Remote Access – Adjust settings, pricing, and promotions anytime.
    • Customer Behavior Insights – Understand preferences and boost engagement.
    • Maximize Profits – Optimize stock and pricing based on live data.

  • AUTOMATED OPERATIONS

    • Engaging Touchscreen Experience – Customers can explore, test, and select perfumes with a digital interface.
    • Smart System – Fully automated for seamless, hands-free operation.
    • Self-Refilling – Detects low levels and alerts for quick restocking.
    • Energy Efficient – Low power consumption saves costs.
    • Effortless Handling – Easy refill and maintenance process.

  • EASY SET-UP

    • Plug & Play Installation – No technical expertise required; get your machine up and running in minutes.
    • Minimal Space Required (0.5m²) – Compact design fits seamlessly into any retail or high-traffic area.
    • Customizable Design – Personalize your Scent Box or Scent Station with your branding, colors, and perfume selections.
    • Hassle-Free Maintenance – Designed for durability with minimal upkeep, ensuring smooth, long-term operation.

  • ADVERTISING & PROMOTIONS
